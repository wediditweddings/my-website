About Us

Where It Began

The Idea Behind
We Did It

We Did It was born from a moment at a friend's wedding — watching a grandmother giggle while filming the first dance on a little camera. That footage was raw, shaky, and completely magical.

Professional videographers capture stunning angles, but they can't capture what Grandma sees. They don't know the inside jokes, the tears behind the smiles, or the way the bride's sister squeezes her hand before she walks out.

That's the footage that makes you ugly-cry and laugh at the same time. That's what we help you get.

  • Founded and operated in Baton Rouge, Louisiana
  • Dedicated to authentic, emotional storytelling
  • Passionate about making wedding films accessible
  • Serving couples across Louisiana and beyond
Our Mission

Making Memories
Accessible to Everyone

Traditional wedding videography can cost thousands of dollars and still miss the unscripted moments that make your day uniquely yours. We wanted to change that.

Our mission is simple: give every couple — regardless of budget — a beautifully edited film that captures the real, human moments no hired camera crew could ever anticipate.

By putting cameras in the hands of your family and friends, we create something no traditional videographer can: a film seen entirely through the eyes of love.
