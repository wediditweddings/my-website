Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks before your wedding date to ensure availability and camera shipping time.

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes. While we're based in Baton Rouge, we ship cameras to couples anywhere in the continental United States.

Do guests need filming experience?

Not at all. Our cameras are user-friendly and come with simple instructions. If you can press a button, you can create beautiful footage.

How is the film delivered?

Your finished 5–7 minute highlight is delivered digitally via download link — easy to save, share, and keep forever.
